USDA Streamline-Assist Program

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) offers loan programs targeted to homeowners with low to moderate household incomes that live in areas designated as rural by the USDA. Providing affordable homeownership opportunities promotes prosperity, which in turn creates thriving communities and improves the quality of life in rural areas.

The USDA Streamline-Assist is a newly expanded refinance loan product with a simplified application process, relaxed eligibility requirements, and dramatically reduced fees.

What is the USDA Streamline-Assist Program?

PennyMac customers can save money every month by lowering the interest rate on their home loan through a refinance. The USDA Streamline Assist loan requirements have been relaxed so it’s easier to qualify and it features a no-hassle application process. Many borrowers who were not eligible for the previous versions of this loan product, or who found its fee structure prohibitive, will be pleased to find they are now eligible and that fees are being dramatically reduced.

Why a USDA Streamline-Assist loan?

Previously, this refi program required a one percent reduction in interest rate but now anyone gaining a minimum $50/month ($600/year) savings can qualify. The initial program was restricted to just 34 states, while the new program is nationwide with a guaranteed savings.

Best of all — as a PennyMac customer — your application process is streamlined for a simplified, quick close:

  • No appraisal needed
  • No credit report
  • No strict FICO score requirements
  • No hassle

More About the Streamline Refinance Program

The Streamline-Assist refinance product is a new USDA Loan Program modeled after a pilot product launched in 2012 for 34 states. That program was not accessible to all borrowers because it contained a requirement that the interest rate be reduced by a minimum of 1%. The new program no longer requires the 1% improvement and, for greater flexibility, instead requires just a tangible benefit to the borrower of $50/month ($600/year) or greater reduction in principal, interest, taxes, and insurance (PITI) including the annual guarantee fee.

The new product is available nationally and is available to PennyMac customers. On an average loan of $165,000, eligible customers could save an extra $2,877 in upfront fees.
